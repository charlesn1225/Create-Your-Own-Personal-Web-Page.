Introduction

Born and raised in Uganda within a refugee camp, my journey, Charles Nshimiyimana, has been one of resilience and diversity. I am currently exploring the expansive world of Information Technology (IT), where my passion for helping others and my love for soccer converge.

My Passion for Soccer and Helping Others

Soccer is not just a game for me; it's a way of life. Playing FIFA and engaging in soccer activities, coupled with my commitment to volunteering at non-profits, forms the core of my interests. This passion seamlessly blends with my IT pursuits. For instance, using my web development skills, I can create platforms for organizing soccer events and enhancing the reach of non-profit organizations. My project, African Youth Ministry, aims to establish a digital community for sharing ideas, stories, and managing activities, thus marrying my IT knowledge with my love for soccer and community service.

Favorite Books

    Harari's exploration of human history and evolution fascinates me, offering insights that parallel my journey in the diverse field of IT.

    The story's message about following one's dreams resonates deeply with me, mirroring my pursuit of goals in soccer and IT.

    Orwell's vision of a dystopian future captivates me, as it contrasts sharply with my aspirations for a world empowered by technology and community.
  5. To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee
    Lee's powerful narrative on justice and moral growth echoes my own values of resilience and making a positive impact in society.
